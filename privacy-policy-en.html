1. How We Collect and Use Your Information

To provide you with stable and secure game services, we may collect the following information:

  • Device and Log Information: To ensure normal game operation, perform data analysis and optimization, we may collect information such as your device model, operating system version, device identifiers, and game crash logs.
  • Game Data: To enable in-game progress saving and feature usage, we collect data such as your game level progress, item collection records, and game settings (e.g., volume, operation preferences).

We use the above information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with game services, and to maintain and improve service quality;
  • To conduct security analysis and error investigation, ensuring the stability of game services;
  • To perform statistical analysis to optimize the game experience, such as understanding user behavior to adjust level difficulty.

2. How We Store Your Information

  • Storage Location: Personal information collected and generated within the People's Republic of China is stored within China.
  • Storage Period: We retain your personal information only for the minimum period necessary to achieve the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. When you delete the game or cancel your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, unless otherwise required by laws and regulations.

3. How We Share, Transfer, and Publicly Disclose Your Information

  • Sharing: We will not share your personal information with third parties unless we obtain your explicit consent, or it is necessary to implement the core functions of this Game (such as integrating compliant third-party analytics SDKs). Where third-party sharing exists, we will clearly disclose the relevant party information and purposes in this Policy.
  • Transfer: We will not transfer your personal information to any third party unless we obtain your prior explicit consent, or in circumstances such as merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, in which case we will notify you in advance.
  • Public Disclosure: We will not publicly disclose your personal information unless required by mandatory provisions of laws and regulations, or with your explicit consent.

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access, Correct, and Delete Personal Information: You may access, correct, or delete your personal information through in-game settings or by contacting our customer service.
  • Withdraw Authorization: You may withdraw your authorization for us to process your personal information at any time. However, withdrawing authorization may affect your ability to use some or all features of this Game.
  • Account Cancellation: If you no longer wish to use this Game, you may apply for account cancellation through in-game settings or by contacting our customer service. After account cancellation, we will delete your relevant information in accordance with legal requirements.

5. Protection of Minors

We attach great importance to the protection of minors' personal information. If you are a minor under the age of 18, please read this Policy under the supervision of a guardian and use this Game with the guardian's consent. If we discover that a minor's personal information has been collected without the guardian's consent, we will immediately cease the relevant activities and delete the data.

6. Information Security

We use industry-standard security technologies and management measures to protect your personal information, preventing data leakage, loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, or tampering.
